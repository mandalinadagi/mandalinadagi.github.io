On the Computation of PSNR for a Set of Images or Video

Authors: Onur Keles, Mustafa Akin Yilmaz, Ahmet Murat Tekalp, Cansu Korkmaz, Zafer Dogan

Venue: Picture Coding Symposium (PCS) 2021, Bristol, United Kingdom, June 29 - July 2, 2021

DOI: 10.1109/PCS50896.2021.9477470

Overview

This paper addresses the proper computation of PSNR metrics for sets of images or video sequences. The work provides methodological insights for accurate quality assessment, discussing different approaches and their implications for fair evaluation of image and video processing algorithms.

Key Contributions

  • Methodological framework for PSNR computation
  • Guidelines for accurate quality assessment
  • Analysis of different computation approaches
