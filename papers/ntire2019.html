NTIRE 2019 Challenge on Video Super-Resolution: Methods and Results

Authors: Seungjun Nah, Radu Timofte, Shuhang Gu, Sungyong Baik, Seokil Hong, Gyeongsik Moon, Sanghyun Son, Kyoung Mu Lee, and others (including Cansu Korkmaz)

Venue: IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Workshops (CVPR Workshops) 2019, Long Beach, CA, USA, June 16-20, 2019

DOI: 10.1109/CVPRW.2019.00250

Overview

This paper presents the methods and results from the NTIRE 2019 Challenge on Video Super-Resolution. The challenge brought together researchers from around the world to develop and benchmark state-of-the-art approaches for video super-resolution, showcasing the latest advances in the field and establishing new benchmarks for future research.

Key Contributions

  • Comprehensive benchmark for video super-resolution
  • State-of-the-art method comparisons
  • Standardized evaluation framework
