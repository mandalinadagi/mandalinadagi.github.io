Fused Glass Art

I am engaged in glass art and have produced several artworks using the glass fusion technique. Glass fusion is a kiln-forming process where separate pieces of glass are heated in a kiln until they soften and bond together, creating unique and beautiful pieces.

Below are some of my glass art creations. Each piece is handcrafted using the glass fusion technique, combining different colors, textures, and forms to create unique artistic expressions.
